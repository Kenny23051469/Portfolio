Kyō Mart Website

About Kyō Mart

Kyō Mart is a Japanese E-Commence website. It is designed to allow customers to view a catalogue of products and add them to the cart. It also features a feedback form which allows users to put in their feedback. The website uses CRUD components to allow admins to add and remove products from the catalogue.

Target Audience

Kyō Mart's target audience would be fans of Japanese culture, snack enthusiasts, or people interested in Japanese products.
